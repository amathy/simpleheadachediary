Data Policy

  • This app will store data locally on your computer and not on a server
  • The data stored will be diary entries containing whether you had a headache or migraine on that day, how severe it was, and whether you took a painkiller or triptan
  • The stored data is unencrypted
  • The app does not store any personally identifiable data anywhere
  • You can delete the data at any time by pressing the delete button
